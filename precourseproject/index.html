Pokemon Go Cabarrus County is a friendly Pokemon Go community in North Carolina based on the Discord and Facebook that covers Concord, Harrrisburg, Kannapolis and surrounding areas. Feel free to join in raids, hang out with us on Community Day, battle in our Silph Arena tournaments & Cabarrus Gym Battle League or just chat about Pokemon with us. Have questions? Ask in #help in our Discord server, chat with us in Messenger or email contact@pogocabco.com
